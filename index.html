DEVCONF 2026 · BUILD TRACK

48-Hour Hackathon Challenge

Calling all developers, students, and innovators — grab a team, pick a problem worth solving, and spend 48 hours turning it into a working build you can demo on the main stage.

Challenge Theme

This year's challenge is "AI & Open Source Innovation" — build a project that applies practical AI to an open-source tool, workflow, or community problem worth solving.

Event Timeline

  • Registration: Team sign-up and check-in opens the challenge.
  • 48-Hour Coding Sprint: Teams build non-stop from kickoff to submission.
  • Mentor Sessions: Rotating check-ins with engineers throughout the sprint.
  • Final Demo Day: Teams present live builds to the judging panel.
  • Winner Announcement: Results and prizes revealed on the main stage.

Grand Prize

Winning teams share a cash prize pool, internship interviews with sponsor companies, limited-edition DEVCONF swag, official certificates, and direct networking time with industry leaders.

Why Join the Hackathon?

Learn from industry mentors guiding your team's build.

Build real-world projects for your portfolio.

Expand your professional network with peers and mentors.

Win exciting prizes and career opportunities.
